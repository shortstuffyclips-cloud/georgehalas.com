Founder • Coach • Owner • NFL pioneer

George Stanley “Papa Bear” Halas

George Halas helped shape pro football from its earliest days—founding and building the franchise that became the Chicago Bears, coaching for decades, and advocating for the sport as the NFL grew into a national institution.
